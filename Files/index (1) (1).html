About FreshSense

FreshSense is engineered to monitor and detect harmful gases in mushroom farming environments, ensuring optimal conditions for growth and safety.

Benefits

Reduces farmer stress by automatically monitoring the farm around the clock.

A healthier environment leads to better-growing, higher-quality mushrooms that sell for a better price.

Contributes to better harvest consistency, fewer disease outbreaks, cleaner grow rooms, and higher income.
